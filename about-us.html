LIBPET, established in 2022, brings together leading researchers and experienced engineers from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, and the University College London. We focus on the research, development, and application of cutting-edge technologies. Our commitment lies in creating a new ecosystem for smart travel, providing high-performance robotic solutions, and spearheading innovations in energy-saving management systems. Our aim is to promote social progress and sustainable development through the power of technology.
